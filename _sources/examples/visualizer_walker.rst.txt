Visualizer Demo: Walker Constellation
=====================================

The `examples/visualizer_walker.py` script demonstrates usage of the :doc:`/api/visualizer` provided with DSNS.
It is simpler than :doc:`/examples/visualizer_demo`, and only runs an example Walker constellation.
